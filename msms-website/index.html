FAQ
Common Questions
Everything a buyer needs before placing an order, presented in a simpler and more premium layout.
Is this really a one-time payment? ▾
Yes. You pay once and the app is yours forever. There are no monthly fees, no renewal charges, and no subscriptions of any kind.
How do I receive the app after payment? ▾
After we verify your payment, we send your license key and download link to the email address you provide. Install the APK on your Android phone and complete the setup steps.
What Android version is required? ▾
The app requires Android 8.0 or higher. It is not currently available for iPhone.
Can I use it on multiple phones? ▾
Each license key activates one account. If you need the app on multiple devices for staff, contact us for a multi-device arrangement.
How long does payment verification take? ▾
Typically within 2-4 hours during business hours, and often faster. You will receive an email as soon as your order is approved.
What payment methods are accepted? ▾
We accept JazzCash, Easypaisa, and direct bank transfer. All payments are one-time only.
